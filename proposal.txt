My program proposal is a simulation of a football (soccer) tournament with 8 teams. The team and player names will be read from external text files,
as well as the players' assigned "power level". There will be 4 time periods for each match (7 matches, 28 time periods) where goals are scored
(2 for each half). A "Man of the Match" will also be picked for each match. The "teams" will be an array of 3 lists, one including the player's names,
one including their corresponding power levels, and one including the team's total power level.
The Man of the Match is based on the winning team and randomised favouring the players with higher power levels.
The goals scored in each period are randomised based on the team's power level, which adds up the player's level, and randomises it to be 80% to 120%
of the total. Each stage of the tournament will be randomised and any teams that lose are erased from the map.
